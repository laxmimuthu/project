Welcome to our college

Savannah College of Arts and Science was established in the year 1965 by Sri. Si.Pa.Kumaran. It is located in the V.K.Puthor village of Surantai Taluk in Thenkasi District of TamilNadu. The founder, Sri.Si.Pa.Kumaran, a native of Kayamozhi Village, wanted to serve his place of birth.He formed a trust called "Kumaran Educational Institution (K.E.I). As the first project, he established college of Arts and Science with the sole aim of providing higher educational opportunities for the youth of this area. His son has been the Founder President of the college committee since its inception.The Savannah college has been affiliated to Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli, since 1990.

Facilities

Our institution Savannah College, has sufficient infrastructural facilities such as class rooms, laboratories, library, browsing Centre, sports complex and many other support facilities.


HOSTAL

The hostel has excellent living rooms, a dining hall, and prayer. The accommodation maintains high standards of hygiene, round the clock security, and provides uninterrupted power and water supply as well.

TRANSPORT

The college runs 10 busses for the convenience of the students living in and around V.K.Puthor. Students are permitted use the service after paying the prescribed bus fees.
